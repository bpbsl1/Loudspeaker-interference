Loudspeaker Interference Demo

Two in-phase speakers create constructive and destructive interference depending on path-length difference.

Physics shown

Constructive interference: Δd = mλ, where m = 0, 1, 2, ...

Destructive interference: Δd = (m + 1/2)λ, where m = 0, 1, 2, ...

Drag the black microphone point to hear/see the changing interference. Use the sliders to change wavelength and speaker spacing.
